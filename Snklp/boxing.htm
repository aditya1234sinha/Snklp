ROBOXING

For Selection:

  • Final round
  • Selection by entry basis
  • Winner will go to next round
  • Direct fight between two bots
  • Repetition of same process until two bots are selected for final round

Bot Size:

  • 30 x 30 x 30cm ( ±10% allowance)
  • Maximum power should not exceed 24 V (DC)
  • Weight of the bot should not be more than 7 kgs (Maximum)

Disciplinary Rule:

  • Objective will be announced on the spot
  • Maximum number of member in a group is 4
  • Decision of the event coordinators will be final and binding
  • College/Institute ID cards are mandatory for registration in the event
  • All the above rules and specifications may change without prior notice
  • Destruction of the arena or any kind of indiscipline may lead to disqualification of the team
