JUNKYARD

Rules and Regulation

For selection:

  • There will be two rounds i.e. Elimination round (consisting of riddles and aptitude and the round will be time based) and the final round.
  • The final round will have the qualified members from the elimination round.
  • The problem statement will be provided on spot along with the junk required.
  • The model must be made of specific weight and size mentioned in the problem statement.
  • The judgement will be based purely on the durability and the realistic feature of the model.
  • Decisions made by the judges will be considered final.
  • Maximum team size of two members
  • School Students are allowed to participate in this event. Participants need to carry their College/School Id along with them.

Prize Money:

1St Position- RS 5,000

2Nd Position- RS 3,000
