ROSOCCER

General Rules:

  • This is a team event
  • A team may have a maximum of four members
  • A team can be disqualified on terms of discipline
  • Decision of the co-ordinators will be deemed final
  • Damage to the arena may lead to disqualification of the team
  • No bot is allowed to pick the ball or grip it within its body

Bot Specifications:

  • Maximum power supply 18V (DC)
  • Machine should be manually controlled
  • Size of the bot: 25x25x25cm (± 5% allowance)
  • Weight of the bot should not exceed 4kgs (maximum)
  • Wire tightening or any such problems will lead to disqualification

Arena Specifications:

  • The goal post will be of dimension: 55x60cm
  • The arena will be in rectangular shape: 260x130 cm
  • Diameter of the ball will be 40mm (Table tennis ball)
  • Diameter of the ball will be 40mm (Table tennis ball)

Scoring:

For First Round:

  • 50x(number of goals scored) will be added
  • 1000 points will be given at the beginning
  • 50x(number of resets taken) will be subtracted
  • Obstacles dimensions will be displayed on site
  • 50x(number of resets taken) will be subtracted

For Final Round:

  • A three minute half time will be given
  • Time limit for each match is 5 minutes
  • A six minute time will be given to score goals/li>
  • The fixture will be decided by the co-ordinators
  • The team with the maximum number of goals will be the winner
  • The team must use the same bot which was used in the first round
  • In case of ball going down the field, it will be given back at the same point where it fell down
  • In case of mechanical or electrical of the bot, timeout of ten minutes will be given (only once)
  • Two teams will play against each other and the wining team will proceed to next level (knock out stage)
  • All the above rules and specifications may change without prior notice. Decision of event Co-ordinators will be final and binding.
