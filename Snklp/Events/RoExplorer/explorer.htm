ROEXPLORER

The winner isn't the one with the fastest bot, it's the one who refuses to lose. Will it be the same with you??
Show your guts and adrenaline at sankalp!

It's time to place your robot in the challenging arena comprising of all possible terrains like sand, pebbles, obstacles, bumpers, bridges, marbles, inclines, sea-saw, dips, cliffs, ramps, banks, etc.

Robot Specifications:

  • Readymade toys car are not allowed
  • The power supply must not exceed 18V DC (Maximum)
  • Maximum weight of the bot must not exceed 4kgs (Maximum)
  • The bots can be powered on-board as well as externally
  • The maximum dimensions of the robot should be 25x25x25 cm (± 5% allowance)
  • In case of external power supply, the wires must be kept long enough to cover the whole track and wire should remain slack during the complete run

Arena Specifications:

  • The track surface and course line may have unevenness
  • There will be an inclination, but this will not exceed 45 degrees
  • Track width is 40 cm normally and it may change at some places
  • The arena will consist of various obstacles along with sand, bumps, water and other multi-terrain features

General Rules:

  • The bot must be controlled manually
  • Scoring rules will be briefed by co-ordinators on spot
  • All students must have their respective college/institute ID cards
  • A participant cannot be a member of 2 different teams in this event
  • Any action violating fair play will lead to immediate disqualification
  • The participants will be provided with 220 volts, 50 Hz standard AC supply
  • This is a team event. The maximum number of participants allowed in a team is 4
  • It should be noted that the main chassis should remain the same throughout the event
  • All the above rules and specifications may change without prior notice. Decision of event Co-ordinators will be final and binding
